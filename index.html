Operations 2015

This year the Operations Team is huddling up for Project Team Meetings, and everyone is invited! Specifics and training will be handled by the RVPs, but here's a list of basics to get you started:

  • When: Project Team Meetings will occur once a month from the time the PSN is issued until the final electrical inspection.
  • What: The meeting will be an opportunity for all the team players to meet and discuss open project items.
  • Where: The new project management conference room.
  • Who: PM team, Legal, BD/Estimating, Engineering, Coordination, Fire Alarm, Low Voltage, PDR, and IT.
